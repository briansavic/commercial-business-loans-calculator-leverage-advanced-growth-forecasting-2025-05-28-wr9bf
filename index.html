Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the growth forecasts?

Our AI-powered forecasting system maintains a 95% accuracy rate, based on historical data analysis and market trends. The model is continuously updated with real-time market data and validated against actual business outcomes.

What types of loans can I compare?

Our calculator supports multiple loan types including term loans, SBA loans, equipment financing, lines of credit, and revenue-based financing. You can compare up to 5 different loan options simultaneously.

How does the payment scheduling work?

Our smart scheduling system analyzes your cash flow patterns and recommends optimal payment frequencies (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or quarterly). It adapts to seasonal fluctuations and revenue cycles.

Yes, we employ bank-level encryption and security protocols. Your data is protected with 256-bit encryption, and we never share your information with third parties without explicit consent.

What support is available?

We offer 24/7 customer support via email and live chat. Our team of financial analysts is available for scheduled consultations to help interpret results and optimize your financing strategy.
